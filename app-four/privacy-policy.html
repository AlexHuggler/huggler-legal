Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Huggler Holdings LLC ("we," "us," or "our") handles data for the App Four application. We are committed to protecting your information with the highest standards of privacy and security.

Section 1

Data Minimization & Privacy-First

We believe your data is private. Our core philosophy is data minimization: we only collect the absolute minimum data necessary to provide our services. Your personal information is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly choose to share it.

Section 2

Information We Do NOT Collect

We are committed to protecting your privacy by not collecting unnecessary data:

  • Personal Data: Your entries, logs, and personal content remain on your device only.
  • Personal Identifiers: We do not require an account, email registration, or login to use this application.
  • Location Data: We do not track or collect your location information.
Section 3

Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with third-party advertisers or data brokers. This app does not contain advertisements. Any analytics we use are anonymized and aggregated to improve app functionality, never to identify individual users.

Section 4

Texas Data Privacy Compliance

We strictly adhere to the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA). As a Texas LLC, we respect your rights to access, correct, and delete your personal data. Your "Right to Delete" is managed by you simply deleting the app or clearing its data from your device.

Section 5

Data Security

Your data is protected using your device's built-in security features, including encryption. We recommend enabling device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID to further protect your information.

Section 6

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and, where appropriate, through in-app notifications.
