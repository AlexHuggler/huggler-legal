Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Huggler Holdings LLC ("we," "us," or "our") handles data for this mobile application. Huggler Holdings LLC is a Texas-based entity dedicated to transparent and secure software development. This policy outlines our commitment to your privacy across our digital products and entrepreneurial endeavors.

Section 1

Data Minimization & Privacy-First

We believe your data is private. Our core philosophy is data minimization: we only collect the absolute minimum data necessary to provide our services. This application is designed so that your personal information never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it.

Section 2

Information We Do NOT Collect

We are committed to protecting your privacy by not collecting unnecessary data:

  • Personal Usage Data: We do not collect, store, or have access to the specific entries, logs, or personal content you create within the app.
  • Personal Identifiers: We do not require an account, email registration, or login to use the features of this application.
Section 3

Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with third-party advertisers or data brokers. Any data retrieved from system-level integrations (such as HealthKit, Calendars, or Location Services) is used strictly for the app's local functionality and is never used for marketing or advertising purposes.

Section 4

Texas Data Privacy Compliance

We strictly adhere to the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA). As a Texas LLC, we respect your rights to access, correct, and delete your personal data. Because we do not store your data on our servers, your "Right to Delete" is managed by you simply deleting the app or its data from your own device.
