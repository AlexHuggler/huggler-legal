Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Huggler Holdings LLC ("we," "us," or "our") handles data for the Bladder Diary mobile application.

1. Data Minimization & Privacy-First

We believe your health data is private. Bladder Diary is designed so that your personal health information (PHI) never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Health Data: We do not collect, store, or have access to any medical entries or diary logs you create. All data is stored locally on your device's internal storage.
  • Personal Identifiers: We do not require an account or login to use the basic features of the app.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not share health data with third-party advertisers or data brokers. If the app uses Apple HealthKit, data retrieved from HealthKit is never used for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Your Consent

By using the app, you consent to this local-only data storage model. You are responsible for the security of your device and the data stored upon it.
